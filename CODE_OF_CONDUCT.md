# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Enginehaus is committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of background or identity.

## Standards

We follow the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/), an industry-standard code of conduct adopted by many open source projects.

**In brief:**
- Be respectful and inclusive
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards others

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers at:
- **Email**: conduct@enginehaus.dev (or via GitHub issues for non-sensitive matters)

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy and security of reporters.
